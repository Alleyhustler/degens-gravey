Synopsis

How will you rise to the occasion?

Clio, the pretty milkmaid had been organising a special day for the past weeks, one to celebrate the anniversary of your first date. But you won't arrive empty handed...

About...

Entre-d’œufs coquilles is a slice-of-life light-hearted text-adventure, in a parser-choice hybrid format (including both text-command interactions and choice-options), created to explore different interactive puzzle mechanics in a parser.

First created in French for La Sens Dessus Dessous jam - which assigned random topics, concepts and engine previously unused by the participant (Adventuron for me) - the game was subsequently translated to English and expended for the Text Adventure Literacy Jam - a yearly competition whose goal is to create text-adventures for new parser players.

Unlike The Roads Not Taken, this was my first "proper" attempt at making a parser game in a parser engine, following the codes and conventions of the format. While it did not rank high in the competition, the game still received praise for its complexity, humour and puzzles.

You can also pet the animals!
