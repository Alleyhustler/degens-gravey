About...

(not so) strangers in the night is a short slice-of-life interactive game made for the ShuffleComp, SeedComp! and the Zach Jam. The premise was based on the assigned song Lola + Joseph by July Talk, uses a POV shift at each passage, and has a character named Zach, to satisfy each jam's constraint.

While the ShuffleComp assigned half-a-dozen songs to each participants, I knew Lola + Joseph was going to be central to my entry. From the first listen, I could see the different scenes clearly in my head. Then it was just a matter of writing everything.

What I liked most about this project was working with this POV shift to tell the story. The flipping back and forth between POV after each passage allowed me to convey more about the characters than I would have otherwise - without feeling like an info dump.
