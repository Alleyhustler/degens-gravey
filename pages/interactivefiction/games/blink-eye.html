About...

In the Blink of an Eye is a short spooky interactive story, created under 4h for the EctoComp, unstyled for the Bare-Bones Jam, and using the INK engine for the inkJam.

In its limited size, the story aims to touch up themes of aimlessness, survival, and fatalism. The game was praise for the size of content considering the limited creation period, and for its mysterious atmosphere.
