Synopsis

Follow Detective Rye, a down-on-the-luck P.I., coming across a very peculiar case. What dark secrets will you uncover?

About...

The Rye in the Dark City is an ongoing noir mystery interactive game, originally created for the SeedComp!.As of now, the demo only includes the first act of the investigation.

The premise was heavily inspired by the noir genre, especially the tropes of the down-on-the-luck P.I., the dame with a dark secret, and the twists and turns during the investigation.
