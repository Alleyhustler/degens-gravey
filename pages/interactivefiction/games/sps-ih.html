Synopsis

Your content life aboard the SPS Iron Hammer is disrupted with the awakening of a new crew member, and his erratic behaviour. A worrisome truth plagues you: the young man is the only person remembering his life prior to his arrival.

About...

SPS Iron Hammer is a relatively short sci-fi mystery interactive story, set aboard the titular spaceship. It was originally created for the Interact-IF Game Jam, under the ranked theme of 'cliffhanger'.

While I do believe in the story behind it, and have a plan on how it could be expanded to satisfy the mystery, I don't think the actual entry didn't manage to meet the theme truly. In my excitement about the premise, I missed the timing to make a great cliffhanger (made it one scene longer than it should have been), which cheapened somewhat the reveal. Yet, I am fond of this game, thanks to the valuable lessons it taught me.

After collecting the feedback from the jam, I doodled some potential paths to complete the story, past the cliffhanger, provide a proper conclusion to the story, and include investigative mechanics. Unfortunately, I haven't been able to get back to this project yet.
