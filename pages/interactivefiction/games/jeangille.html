Les lettres du Docteur Jeangille

Dr Jeangille's Letters

Synopsis

Forced to settle in the quiet village of Meaux, the life of Doctor Jeangille falls into turmoils. Exchanging with Olympia, the lover, this recluse person comes to life, entangled in peculiar and mysterious events.

Write letters to the beloved you were forced to leave behind. Re-discover your hometown. Uncover a mystery. Take your fate in your own hands.

About...

Les Lettres du Docteur Jeangille, also known as Doctor Jeangille's Letters is an epistolary interactive game made in Twine for the 2024 Concours de Fiction Interactive Francophone, then translated into English for the following SpringThing.

The game played the themes of the Concours, which were Illusion and Venu d'Ailleurs (stranger), as it unravels Jeangille's discoveries (or lack thereof). Part slice-of-life romance and mystery, the story is a love letter to melodramatic 19th century stories and the forbidden fruit.
