Free-to-Use Prompts

Ideas and inspirations for all!
Sporadic releases

Since the SeedComp!, I have been submitting prompts to different jams, as another outlet for creative writing. Every prompt followed the rules of their respective jams, whether it be themes or constraints. Some are multi-linguals, some only available in English.

Any user is welcome to download and use them as they wish. They are available under the CC-BY licence unless indicated otherwise.

Click on a title to learn more about the prompt!
